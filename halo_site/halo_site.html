Events leading up to the Human Covenant War

The setting of Halo takes place 500 years in the future when humanity has become largely united forming the UNSC United Nations Space Command, has formed colonies on other worlds, and figured out how to travel faster than light. During this time they had to deal with various rebellions and uprisings. Because of this ONI ake the Office of Naval Intelligence started the Spartan Program to ensure that the UNSC and its colonies would remain stable. This was done by sending out operatives into the field to kidnap various children who met the requirements. They were then taken to the planet Reach where they would be trained and indoctrinated. Years later they would be given augmentations to increase their reflexes, speed, and strength. They would be equipped with Mjolner power armor which would further augment their speed and strength, as well as give them the ability to interface with ai. In the year 2525 a Covenant ship crewed by Jackals would raid an UNSC vessel. At this time humans had no evidence of intelligent alien life and just found numerous planets with animals so nobody thought it was aliens. They just thought it was rebels or pirates. Later they hid some troops onboard a ship that acted as bait and encountered the aliens. They would later make contact with another ship headed by Brutes and meet at the planet Harvest to form a trade agreement/peace treaty. The Covenant used a device called a Luminary that detected Forerunner artifacts and found the human colony was covered with relics. A battle broke out when a Grunt accidentally attacked a human soldier. There was then an extended engagement of Harvest between the Covenant detachment and the humans on the planet. Back at the mobile fortress/home world High Charity the three who would later become the prophets of Truth, Mercy and Regret asked this ancient Forerunner ai Mendicant Bias about the artifacts that had the glyph reclamation. The ai told them they were grossly misunderstanding and turned the glyph upside down where it then said reclaimer. Reclaimer was the Forerunner symbol for humanity and meant that their descendants would reclaim all the lost technology after life was reseeded following the Rings being fired to stop the Flood. They also learned that not everyone who believed in their faith would be saved by the Halos firing, and that some were left behind. Upon realizing that their faith was a lie and that humanity were the descendants/inheritors of the Forerunner’s legacy they declared a war of extermination to wipe this newly discovered species out. This would preserve their political power and keep the masses inline preventing the dissolvement of the Covenant. Thus beginning the Human-Covenant War. Later additions to the lore would contradict this by making Forerunners and humans two different species in this very convoluted book trilogy.

Major events of the war and the parties involved

The first major battle of the war would be the attack on Harvest that was previously mentioned. The UNSC was able to delay this new alien force just long enough to get most of the population off world. Eventually the Covenant would just fire superheated plasma from their ships that turned the surface into volcanic glass. The Covenant almost never landed troops on the ground unless they had a really good reason. In ground engagements the UNSC were able to hold their own and do a lot of damage to the Covenant ground forces. Space was an entirely different matter because the Covenant ships had shields, plasma torpedoes that would home in on their targets melting through the ship’s hull, and greatly outnumbered the human ships. Whenever they’d show up it wasn’t a question of “how do we win?”, it was always “how do we delay the Covenant for long enough to get enough of the population off world before they destroy everything?”. Since the Covenant’s goal was extermination and could just terraform the plants later they would just glass them and move on to the next one. This brutal campaign went on for 28 years with only earth remaining as the last human population center. Since the Luminaries would detect Forerunner relics and viewed humans as Forerunner relics, they would be able to detect human planets. During this time a man by the name of Preston Cole who was the Vice Admiral was given control of the largest human fleet to combat the new alien threat. Under his leadership they were able to take back Harvest in 2526, and engaged the Covenant around the outer colonies for about 5 years. The Covenant then returned to Harvest resulting in a brutal 5 year conflict that ended with humans taking back the planet. Around this time the Covenant’s leadership realized that devoting their fleets to this war of extermination would leave them vulnerable to a potential attack from any unknown alien powers/species. In 2552 the Covenant’s solution was to use Forerunner technology to build a new fleet that they could use to win the war fast. This kicks off the events of the rts Halo Wars 1 where the Spirit of Fire and it’s crew would investigate a Forerunner structure that the Covenant took interest in. This contained a star chart that would lead the Covenant to the human colony Arcadia. At the time there was an Arbiter who captured a human scientist because she was able to use Forerunner technology and they would be able to use her to activate their prize. The Spirit of Fire chased the Covenant to a shield world that was a Flood infection on it’s surface. Eventually the planet opened up and brought the Spirit of Fire inside. ONce inside they rescued the scientist and used their slipspace drive to detonate the shield world. This victory prevented humanity from being wiped out then and there. But it resulted in them being stranded in space. The crew then went into cryosleep until they were needed. By the year 2534 most of the outer colonies had been glassed by the Covenant. Some of these worlds included important agricultural and manufacturing worlds. Because of the problems caused by this the UNSC imposed all kinds of restrictions of travel, rationing out food, and other necessary precautions. This was to prevent more colonies from being discovered. The Insurrectionists not believing the Covenant were real or at least just thinking it was grossly exaggerated propaganda began to start fighting the UNSC more than they were before. These rebels sided with what they thought were Jackal renegades who were trading weapons with them. At the time the rebels were unaware of the fact that these modified weapons had tracking devices in them and the Jackals wanted to take the rebel hideout for themselves. The UNSC learned about this and were horrified because of the security risk, while the Elites were angered that this unholy species would dare modify their weapons. THis hideout was then destroyed. The years 2536-2551 would be referred to as the Siege of the Inner Colonies because the Covenant had finally pushed through into the most important sections of human space. Several important worlds would be destroyed with a few costly victories for the UNSC. One of these was referred to as Admiral Cole’s last stand where he lured 200 Covenant ships into a gas giant and detonated it with some nukes temporarily turning the planet into a star. He was assumed to be dead and is revered as a hero for his actions. In 2552 ONI devised the plan Operation Red Flag where they planned to locate the Covenant’s home world so they could capture a high prophet and force the Covenant to a truce. All of the Spartan 2s would be brought back to the planet Reach, which was the most important planet second only to earth. The Covenant would find this planet and began a siege that was very costly for both sides. At the time it was believed that all Spartans aside from Master Chief had died in the battle. As the planet was being destroyed, the human ship the Pillar of Autumn made a slipspace jump based on some alien coordinates and found a Halo Ring beginning the events of the first game. On the Ring since it was a holy relic that the Covenant believed would allow them to attain salvation they were unable to use their usual glassing tactics because that would destroy/damage the Ring. On the surface of the Ring the crew were able to do lots of damage to the Covenant through guerilla tactics. Eventually the Covenant accidentally released this fungal parasite called the Flood that had destroyed the Forerunners thousands of years ago. The Ring’s ai monitor led Chief to the control center and tried to get him to activate the Ring to destroy the Flood. Luckily the ai Cortana told Chief that the Rings kill all organic life in the area to starve the Flood. They then had to destroy the Ring by detonating the Autumn’s engines. In the second game after escaping with a group of marines Chief and the other survivors from the Ring were having an award ceremony right as a small Covenant fleet led by the Prophet of Regret appeared right outside of earth’s defenses. At the same time the Elite commander who was incharge of the fleet that followed them to the Ring was punished for his failure. Chief and everyone else would beat back the attack and they were about to board Regret’s ship to engage operation red flag. The ship jumped and so they followed him to another Halo. At the same time the Elite who was shamed was given the title Arbiter by the Prophet of Truth because he was still useful. He was then sent to wipe out some Elite heretics who learned the truth about the Rings. On the Ring Chief would kill Regret causing the Brutes to switch places with the Elites becoming the Prophet’s personal guards. This caused tensions to rise and eventually the Brutes would turn on the Elites causing the Great Schism. This would cause the Elites to side with humans out of desperation and because they learned the Rings would kill everyone. Chief would be on High Charity at the time and was chasing Truth and Mercy. The Flood had escaped and began infecting High Charity. The Arbiter and some humans would travel to earth and Chief would board Truth’s ship and travel to earth. In 3 the Covenant fleet had finally made their war to earth in search of the Ark. The Ark was the place where the Rings were built and could fire them all from there. By this time humans and Elites had formed an alliance and were working together against the Covenant. The Brutes had taken on new roles they hadn’t had before and were the new leaders of the Covenant military. A Flood infected ship made its way to earth and a group of Elite ships had chased it so the infection wouldn’t spread. The combined fleet then went through the portal that led to the Ark where they fought Truth’s forces. Eventually Truth was killed by the Arbiter effectively ending the war since the leadership was gone. Chief and the Arbiter would then escape and since Chief was in the back part of the ship that was cut off he was left drifting in space.
